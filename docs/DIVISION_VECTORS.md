## Derived Metrics and Details
- **Back Width**: Represents linear axillary breadth divided by height (not a circumference). Men's Open aims for maximum lat spread target.
- **Shoulder-to-Waist**: Calculated as the ratio of shoulder circumference to waist circumference.
- **V-Taper**: A weighted aesthetic ratio; Men's Open, Classic, and Men's Physique share the maximum 1.618 ideal V-taper parameter despite having varying absolute sizes.
